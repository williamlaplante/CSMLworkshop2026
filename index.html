This workshop will bring together researchers at the forefront of computational statistics to discuss recent advances in the field. Topics will include Monte Carlo methods, gradient flows, simulation-based inference and variational inference. This workshop is scheduled alongside the UCL Institute of Mathematics and Statistical Sciences (IMSS) annual lecture.
